The Process

Your Journey in 6 Simple Steps

Step 01
📋

Create Your Account

New to Ghar Seva? Sign up in under 60 seconds with your name, email, and phone number. No OTP wait loops, no unnecessary forms — just quick and clean onboarding.

✨ Free Forever
1
Step 02
🔐

Log In Securely

Already have an account? Log in with your email and password. Your session is saved so you don't have to log in every time — your profile, bookings, and preferences stay intact.

🔒 Secure Login
2
Step 03
🔧

Browse & Pick a Service

Head to the Services page and filter by category — Plumbing, Electrician, AC Repair, Cleaning, and more. Each service shows ratings, pricing, and estimated arrival time so you always know what you're getting.

📍 12+ Categories
3
🔒

Login Required to Book

To protect you and our workers, booking is only available to logged-in users. If you're browsing as a guest, you'll be redirected to login before confirming any booking. It takes less than a minute!

Step 04
⭐

Choose Your Expert

We show you available workers near you, ranked by work experience and customer ratings. Browse their profiles, see past reviews, and pick the professional you trust most — the choice is always yours.

🏆 Verified Professionals
4
Step 05
📅

Pick a Time Slot

Select a date and time slot that works for you. The worker gets notified instantly and must accept the booking within minutes. If they're unavailable or decline, you're shown other top-rated alternatives — no dead ends.

⚡ Instant Confirmation

Step 06
💳

Pay Online — Done!

Once the job is accepted, pay securely online via UPI, card, or net banking — no cash handling, no awkward change conversations. After the work is done, rate your experience to help the community.
